NightScape Website





NightScape, was my first ever project, working with html and css!
It is a landing page based of the chrome extention Momentum. The backgound switches according to the time
of day and you can dynamically put in your name and your focus for today! It is currently made on
a website, but i would like to make it into an extention!



How to use


1. Go to https://mountaintiger144.github.io/NightScape/
2. Fill in your name in the [Name] section
3. Also every day, fill in your main focus for that day!
4. Restart google and you should see the info still there!
